About Us

FemiBloom Yoga provides support and strength through every stage of your journey — trying to conceive, pregnancy, and postpartum.

  • Prenatal Yoga: Designed for expectant mothers to help you stay fit, reduce stress, and prepare for birth.
  • Postnatal Yoga: Restorative and strengthening practices to help heal and reconnect with your body.
  • Womb Wisdom: A modern take on Garbhasanskar — using music, affirmations, yoga, and mindfulness to nurture your baby in the womb.
