About FemiBloom

FemiBloom is a warm, judgment-free sanctuary for all women, embracing every journey of motherhood—before, during, and after. We believe yoga is for every body, not every "Instagram body".

Our Garbha Harmony sessions bring ancient wellness (Garbh Sanskar) into the modern world with guided audio, affirmations, and gentle movement.
